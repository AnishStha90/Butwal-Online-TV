About Us हाम्रो बारेमा

Butwal Today Online TV is the official digital video and online television platform operated by Butwal Publication Pvt. Ltd., based in Butwal-6, Rupandehi, Nepal. It delivers news bulletins, interviews, reports, highlights, and feature stories focusing on Lumbini Province while also covering national and international affairs. The organization also operates the Butwal Today National Daily newspaper and its digital news portal.

Our Mission हाम्रो मिसन

To deliver accurate, timely, and impartial news and information, educate and inform the public, promote social awareness, and connect communities through responsible journalism and digital media.
